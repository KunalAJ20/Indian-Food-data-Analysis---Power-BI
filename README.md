## Here is the Project Overview
This repository contains a comprehensive Power BI dashboard developed for **Poha Solutions Pvt. Ltd.** to analyze the culinary preferences, dietary distributions, and preparation efficiencies of Indian cuisine. 

## Key Insights
1. **Macro Distribution:** Analyzed recipe volumes across various regional cuisines and course types.

2. **Dietary Analysis:** Evaluated the percentage split between Vegetarian, Non-Vegetarian, and Diabetic-Friendly options.

3. **Efficiency Metrics:** Identified that 30 minutes is the most frequent total time across the dataset, representing 2,395 recipes.

4. **Niche Tracking:** Isolated specific regional profiles (Andhra Cuisine) and specific health-focused ingredients (Bitter Gourd).

## Files Included
* `Indian_Food_Analysis Screen Shot - The core Power BI dashboard.
* Indian Food DataSet - The raw data set used for analysis.

## Visuals Implemented
* **Clustered Bar/Column Charts:** Cuisine breakdown, course popularity, and time distributions.
* **Pie Charts:** Dietary preferences.
* **Advanced Visuals:** DAX-driven card filters for ingredient searches and Top-N tables for efficiency tracking.
* **Card** : To show the Average time taken and Count of items

## Results

1. **Culinary and Dietary Distribution** - It shows the specific preferences Veg vs Non-Veg and identifies the main course as the most populated meal category

2. **Time and Efficiency Analysis** - It revealed that 30 min is the most frequent total time across the dataset , representing 2395 recipes.It shows that indian cuisine is highly adaptable fro quick and easy meal prep marketing.

3. **Targeted Segment Tracking - Used precise cataloging for Andhra Cuisine and used custom DAX string to pull the exact count of recipes using bitter gourd.It allows the company to track the exact number.
